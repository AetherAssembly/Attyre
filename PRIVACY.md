# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026 · Attyre by AetherAssembly

> **Short version:** Attyre collects nothing. Your wardrobe data never leaves your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing of any kind.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Attyre is a free wardrobe management app developed and maintained by AetherAssembly. You can reach us at [support@aetherassembly.org](mailto:support@aetherassembly.org) or via our [contact form](https://forms.gle/T4i7GGzaT3HUrffm9).

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on our servers — because we don't have any servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any identifying information
- Your location or IP address
- Your wardrobe items, photos, or notes
- Usage data, analytics, or behavioural telemetry
- Cookies of any kind

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

Attyre stores the following data locally — on your device only:

- Your clothing items (names, categories, tags, warmth levels, notes)
- Any photos you attach to items
- Saved outfit combinations and calendar plans
- App preferences (dark mode, accessibility mode)

Data is stored in your OS app data directory (`%APPDATA%\Attyre` on Windows, `~/.config/Attyre` on Linux, `~/Library/Application Support/Attyre` on macOS). Photos are saved as image files in an `images/` subfolder. Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete this folder.

This data is entirely under your control. You can export it, delete it, or wipe it at any time from the Settings page. There is no cloud backup.

## 4. Third-Party APIs

When you use the **Suggest Outfit** or **Pack** features, Attyre makes outbound requests to fetch weather data. No personal data is sent — only the city name you type:

- **OpenStreetMap Nominatim** ([nominatim.openstreetmap.org](https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org)) — converts a city name to coordinates. Subject to the [Nominatim Usage Policy](https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/nominatim/).
- **Open-Meteo** ([open-meteo.com](https://open-meteo.com)) — fetches current temperature and weather conditions for those coordinates. Subject to the [Open-Meteo Terms](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms).

Both are free, open services with no affiliation to AetherAssembly. Their own privacy policies apply to any data they may collect from API requests.

If you do not use the Suggest Outfit or Pack features, no outbound requests are made at all.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Attyre is a general-purpose tool with no age restrictions. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, there is nothing specific to disclose regarding children's privacy.

## 6. Your Rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete on our end. To manage or delete your own data, use the Export and Import tools in the app's Settings page.
